Clean Gym

Is a educational portuguese browser game, which addresses one of the issues a primary school had, which is that their gyms were always dirty.

So our goal was to show that if everyone does their part, the gym will always be clean, teaching the kids that cooperation and duty is essential.

The game also won awards at the MILT Conference.
