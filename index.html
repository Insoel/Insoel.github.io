About

Hello, I'm Ianis Arquissandas, and I am a Game Developer and Programmer in Portugal.
I'm currently at my last year in college, taking a Videogame Bachelor degree, at Universidade Lusofona.
I've been in love with games since I can remember, as such I decided to pursue a career related to games.
At 12 I started making custom maps and messing around with models for some of Valve's games, using their Hammer software. I also learned quite a bit of blender around that age.
In college I found my passion in programming video games. I learned as much as I could using the Unity Engine, I also learned some programming languages, such as some basic knowledge in C and JavaScript and a very extensive knowledge of C# which is my main programming language.
I studied design patterns, databases and artificial inteligence for video games.
