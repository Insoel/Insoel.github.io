Joaquim, the Architect

Joaquim, the Architect

This was a game we did in my second year for a elementary school with deaf kids, we had to create a game which would help them in solving and learning mathematical questions and formulas.

By doing so the game allows them to unlock buildings and place them where they desire.

Story

You are Joaquim, one day you wake up and you're an architect, you get to build your own little city, you have to solve mathematical problems to unlock your buildings.
